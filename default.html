Magic Leap↑

Magic leap is supposedly building goggles that can change peoples preception of reality. In last few years it has become one of the most mysterious and exciting companies in tech, and they released their first developer version Magic Leap One.

Magic Leap One is one of the most awaited augmented reality smart glass, which got heavily funded by Google and Alibaba Group in 2016 and on August 8th, 2018 Magic leap released there first developer kit called the Magic Leap One. Magic Leap is a gadgets that help people stay present in the real world, while simultaneously letting them reap the benefits of high-powered, always-connected technology.
